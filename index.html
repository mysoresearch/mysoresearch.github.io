About

Mysoresearch is an independent platform dedicated to financial technical analysis. We study price behaviour, volume dynamics, and market microstructure to develop systematic, repeatable frameworks for understanding market cycles.

Our work spans Indian equity markets (NSE/BSE), commodity futures, and global indices, with a focus on actionable pattern recognition and indicator-driven research — not tips or speculation.
